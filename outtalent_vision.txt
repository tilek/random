DRAFT

We think a great world is where every person knows what their full potential is and can reach it.
[Basic skills] Every person has basic skills (e.g. critical thinking) and knowledge (e.g. English).
[Self-belief] Every person understands they can accomplish world-changing things.
[Ikigai] Every person understands their unique traits and how they can use them to help others.
[Potential] Every person can reach their full potential and have the maximum positive impact on the world if they want to.
