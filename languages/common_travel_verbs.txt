3. LIVING BEINGS WITH BASIC INTELLIGENCE APPEARED

HEAD-RELATED ACTIONS
- sleep
- look [focus eyes but not necessarily register]
- see [register with eyes in a short or long amount of time] (e.g. did you see any waterfalls there)
- watch [register with eyes for a long amount of time] (e.g. can I watch my movie in this room tomorrow)
- hear
- listen

THOUGHTFUL ACTIONS WITH THINGS OR PEOPLE
- make
- use (e.g. can I use your bathroom)

KNOWLEDGE & ABILITIES
- can/able [can accomplish a certain goal]
- think [process in the brain]
- know [have certain information]
- forget [lose certain information]
- understand [have a logical model]
- learn [develop a logical model] (e.g. where did you learn your English)
- study [develop logical models using a system] (e.g. where did you study)

FOOD
- drink
- eat

PREFERENCES, OPINION, PERCEPTION
- feel
- want
- need
- like
- love
- look [appearance] (e.g. human looks a bit like a chimp, you look young, it looks beautiful)

MOVING THINGS OR LIVING BEINGS

- bring (e.g. can you bring some water please)

- meet [exchange information about each other]
- recommend

- buy
